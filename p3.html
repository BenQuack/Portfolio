Project Description

For this project I created a simple video game using the SFML library. The gameplay was just moving around a square to doge enemies/obstacles. The enemies were a circle that bounced around off the walls with a small amount of randomization and player tracking added to the bouce calculations after a random amount of time the enemies would attack by staying still and 'impacting' the ground. you lost the game if you were hit by an enemy.
